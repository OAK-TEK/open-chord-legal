# Privacy Policy

**Open Chord**
**Effective Date:** July 15, 2026
**Developer:** OAK TEK - FZCO

This Privacy Policy describes how OAK TEK - FZCO ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the Open Chord mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Age Requirement

Open Chord is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to remove that information.

## Information We Collect

Open Chord does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal contact information.

### Data Stored on Your Device

All of your learning progress, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using the App's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option found in the Guitarist tab under My Data.

### Microphone Access

The App includes an instrument tuner that requires access to your device's microphone to detect pitch in real time. All audio captured by the microphone is analyzed entirely on your device using on-device signal processing. No audio is recorded, stored, cached, or transmitted to any server, third party, or external service at any time. Microphone access is used solely for the tuner feature and for no other purpose. You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings without affecting any other functionality in the App.

### Analytics Data

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used in aggregate. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect certain information, including:

- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- General usage patterns (such as which features are used and how often)
- Crash and performance data
- Anonymous app-instance identifiers

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to personally identify you. It helps us understand how users interact with the App so we can improve the learning experience.

For more information on how Google handles this data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

### Advertising Data

Open Chord displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support the App's free access model. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, including:

- Device advertising identifier
- General location (city-level, derived from IP address)
- Device and network information
- Ad interaction data (such as whether an ad was viewed or tapped)

You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads
- **iOS:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

For more information on how AdMob handles data, please refer to Google's Advertising Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

## How We Use Information

We use the information described above solely to:

- Provide and maintain the App
- Understand usage patterns and improve features
- Display advertisements to support the App's free access model
- Monitor app stability and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

- **Google Firebase Analytics:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Google AdMob:** https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information directly, there is no personal data for us to retain. Analytics and advertising data are handled by Google in accordance with their data retention policies. Data stored locally on your device remains there until you use the "Clear All Data" option within the App, clear the App's data through your device settings, or uninstall the App.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Request information about what data has been collected
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of personalized advertising

Since Open Chord does not collect personally identifiable information, most data requests can be addressed by using the "Clear All Data" option in the App, adjusting your device's advertising settings as described above, or clearing the App's data through your device settings. For any additional requests, please contact us.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with the App. Since all personal learning data is stored locally on your device, its security is primarily managed by your device's own security features. No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.
